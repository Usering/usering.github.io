About Me

Hi I'm Adam Schneider. Cars and computers interest me.

Growing up in Silicon Valley always gave me wonder about computers and just how software works. This always gave me something to do after school and kept me wondering (and staying out of trouble). In 2012, I finally learned how to code on a game I like to call ROBLOX. 4 years later, I have made enough money doing what I love on ROBLOX to buy myself my dream car. When I was 6, my brother began working on his '62 Chop-Top Bug. It was red and came already chopped. I used to come out and help my dad and my brother out when they would work on it- and this also gave me interest in how a car works. Now that I'm 16, I finally get to choose my own car and work on it with my dad to make it exactly how I want it.
